Your Cycle

Four phases.
One you.

Your cycle is more than a period — it's a dynamic hormonal system with four distinct phases, each with its own energy, mood, and opportunities for your body.

Days 1–5 · Menstrual

Rest & Restore

The shedding phase. Low energy is natural and intentional — prioritize gentle movement, iron-rich foods, and deep rest.

Days 6–13 · Follicular

Energy Rises

Estrogen climbs. Creativity and motivation surge — the perfect time for new projects, social activities, and harder workouts.

~Day 14 · Ovulation

Peak Confidence

Peak estrogen and testosterone. You're at your most energetic, magnetic, and communicative. Lean into it fully.

Days 15–28 · Luteal

Prepare & Wind Down

Progesterone rises. Focus on nourishing foods, mindfulness, and lighter exercise as your body prepares for the next cycle.
